Regime Autoresearch + Meta-Harness

A two-loop autonomous optimization system for Nifty 50 options regime classification
INNER LOOP (Autoresearch) Optimizes the classifier
OUTER LOOP (Meta-Harness) Optimizes the frame
⚡ What Is This?

This is a two-loop autonomous research system that optimizes a regime classification engine for three Nifty 50 options selling strategies: DM (Delta-Managed), WC (Wide-Condor), and Orion.

The inner loop (Autoresearch) modifies the classification logic — IV boundaries, feature selection, strategy weights — to maximize a composite score on validation data. Over 109 experiments it improved the score from 0.568 to 0.926.

The outer loop (Meta-Harness) optimizes the frame the inner loop operates in — the scoring formula weights, normalization constants, feature pipeline, and train/val split. After each inner loop completes, the meta-harness evaluates the best classifier on truly held-out OOS data that the inner loop never sees. This prevents overfitting and finds configurations that genuinely generalize.

💡 Why Two Loops?
The frame shapes the solution
The inner loop can only explore within the search space defined by the scoring formula. If the formula gives 40% weight to Sharpe and 25% to safe separation, the agent finds the best solution within that frame. But what if rank stability deserves 30% weight? A different scoring formula would lead the inner loop to discover a completely different — potentially better — classifier.
Validation ≠ generalization
The inner loop achieved 0.926 on validation, but the same classifier scored Sharpe 0.00 on the most recent OOS data (Feb–Mar 2026). The meta-harness exists to find scoring configurations that produce classifiers that actually generalize to unseen market conditions.

🎯 Scoring Formula (meta-harness tunable)
composite = w_sharpe × min(val_sharpe / sharpe_norm, sharpe_cap) // default: 0.40 × min(x/5.0, 1.5) + w_safe_sep × min(safe_sep / safe_sep_norm, safe_sep_cap) // default: 0.25 × min(x/10.0, 1.0) + w_rank_corr × max(rank_corr, 0) // default: 0.20 + w_coverage × coverage // default: 0.15 // Purple values are controlled by meta_config.json // The inner loop sees a fixed formula; the outer loop changes it between iterations

Train: Feb 2023 – Jun 2025 (~594 days)  |  Validation: Jul 2025 – Jan 2026 (143 days)  |  OOS: Jan 2021 – Jan 2023 + Feb–Mar 2026 (reserved for meta-harness only)

⚡ 5 Upgrade Systems

Inspired by RD-Agent(Q) and FinRL-X. Each system addresses a specific weakness in the base two-loop architecture.

Upgrade 1
Knowledge Forest
Persistent experiment memory across all meta-iterations. The inner loop reads a summary before starting — avoids re-trying known failures, builds on successes.
forest_manager.py • experiment_forest.json
Upgrade 2
MAB Scheduler
UCB1 multi-armed bandit across 5 dimensions. Adaptively allocates budget to whichever dimension produces the largest OOS improvement.
mab_scheduler.py • mab_state.json
Upgrade 3
IC Dedup Gate
Pairwise Spearman correlation check on activated features. Drops any feature with |corr| > 0.75 to another kept feature before the inner loop starts.
ic_dedup.py • threshold: 0.75
Upgrade 4
Strategy Co-Opt
Alternates between classifier and strategy optimization. The outer loop injects fixed [DM,WC,Orion] weights; the inner loop optimizes only boundaries and features.
strategy_coopt.py • 6 templates
Upgrade 5
Paper Trade Gate
Forward-walk validation on the most recent unseen data. Checks Sharpe, state diversity, and transition stability before promoting to production.
paper_trade_gate.py • PROMOTE/ITERATE/REJECT

💡 Key Insights
WC is king in low IV
The Wide Condor strategy produced a Sharpe of 14.57 in L1 Safe (IV < 8.5). Pure positive theta extraction in calm markets. This single insight accounted for ~50% of the inner loop's score improvement.
Boundaries matter more than features
Compressing IV boundaries from [12,17] to [8.5,11] was the first major breakthrough. Spreading days across all 8 states fixed coverage and rank stability simultaneously.
OOS reveals the truth
The inner loop's 0.926 classifier has OOS1 Sharpe 4.38 (2021–2023) but OOS2 Sharpe 0.00 (Feb–Mar 2026). The classifier works in historical data but fails on the most recent period. This is exactly what the meta-harness is designed to fix.
What didn't work in the inner loop
Adaptive boundaries, dual-snapshot fusion, custom risk features as splitters, VRP-based direction, and WC-heavy weights in L2/L3 all hurt the composite score. WC's edge is specific to low-IV regimes.
